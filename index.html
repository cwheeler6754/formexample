About Team Ghostbusters

Team Ghostbusters was formed in 1990 with seven athletes competing in track & field, swimming, and gymnastics. Since that time, the team has grown to a large family consisting of hundreds of athletes and volunteers. Team Ghostbusters strives to work together to complete fundraising tasks and other needs within the community. New athletes and families are always welcome! Friendships and memories are made to last a lifetime. We have learned from our past, celebrate the present, and look forward to the future!

Sports

The sports we currently offer are:
Summer (March-June) - Basketball, Bowling (Social), Flag Football and Track & Field
Fall (July-October) - Bocce, Bowling (Social & Competitive), Golf and Softball
Winter (December-March) - Alpine Skiing, Floor Hockey and Snowboarding
